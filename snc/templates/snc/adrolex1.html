History of Rolex Submariner

The Rolex Submariner was first introduced in 1953, marking a major milestone in the world of diving watches. Over the decades, it has evolved to incorporate Rolex's latest innovations in waterproofing and precision, while maintaining its status as a symbol of luxury and performance.
