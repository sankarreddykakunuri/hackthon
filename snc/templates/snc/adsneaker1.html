History of Nike Air Jordans

The Nike Air Jordan sneaker line was first introduced in 1984 and revolutionized both basketball and sneaker culture. Designed for Michael Jordan, the Air Jordans set new standards for performance, comfort, and style. The early 1990s models are particularly celebrated for their bold designs and are considered iconic in the world of sports and fashion.
