Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place a bid?

To place a bid, navigate to the auction item page, enter your bid amount, and click "Place Bid." Ensure you are logged in to your account.

2. What happens if I win an auction?

If you win an auction, you will receive a notification with payment instructions. You must complete the payment within the given time frame to finalize the purchase.

3. Can I cancel a bid?

Bids cannot be canceled once placed. Please double-check your bid before confirming.

4. How do I contact the seller?

You can contact the seller through the "Contact Seller" button on the item's auction page. Messages are monitored to ensure compliance with our policies.
