History of Gibson Les Paul

The Gibson Les Paul is one of the most iconic electric guitars in history, first introduced in the early 1950s. Its rich tone, incredible sustain, and classic design have made it a favorite among legendary musicians such as Jimmy Page, Slash, and Eric Clapton. The 1970s models are particularly prized for their craftsmanship and unique tonal qualities.
